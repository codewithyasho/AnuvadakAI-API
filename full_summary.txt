**Title: "Tata Sumo: The Rugged Symbol of Resilience and Endurance"**

- **Introduction of the Tata Sumo**: Launched as a rugged, purpose-built vehicle during an era of primitive roads.
- **Design Philosophy**: Engineered for strength and endurance rather than luxury or speed.
- **Purpose**: Built to conquer long distances and tough terrains, embodying resilience.
- **Symbolism**: Represented more than just a car—it stood as a symbol of determination, mirroring the spirit of the nation it served.
